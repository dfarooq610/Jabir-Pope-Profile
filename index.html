Justice for Jabir Pope

Resilience & Restoring Life After Wrongful Conviction

Joseph Jabir Pope is 70 years old.

Born and raised in Boston, he lives with his siblings on the street they grew up on. He's the father of one daughter and cares deeply about his community.

Jabir enjoys expressing himself through music and poetry. His mind is still sharp and his lyrical flow, unchallenged. Some days, he invites his friends over to rehearse in the attic.

But an unexpected twist in his 30s highlights a reality many in the U.S. face – Jabir spent 38 years incarcerated for a crime everyone agrees he did not commit.
